## Portfolio summary

OrbitOps is a production-oriented multi-agent SaaS platform that converts leads into researched, human-approved, delivered, and auditable business actions. It combines resumable LangGraph workflows, multi-LLM routing, tenant-scoped RBAC, communication delivery intelligence, immutable audit records, PDF reports, and an enterprise AI Operations console.
